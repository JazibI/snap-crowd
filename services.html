Challenges Facing:

- Non-user-friendly website restricting lead generation opportunities.

possible Solution:

- Revamp the website to make it more user-friendly and conducive to lead generation.

- Integrate demand for Zufta Travel & Tours' services through digital marketing channels.

- Provide support and training to the sales team to effectively manage digitally generated leads.

- Launch social media campaigns across search engines, YouTube, Snapchat, Facebook, and Instagram to increase engagement and reach.

- Implement aggressive retargeting messaging strategies to drive demand generation and conversion.

- Proactively embrace digital marketing strategies to overcome existing challenges and enhance business growth in Pakistan's travel and tourism industry.

MOVENOW

MOVENOW

movenow is a physiotherapy and rehabilitation center in Pakistan dedicated to providing quality care and services to patients.

With a focus on enhancing the well-being and health of individuals, MOVENOW offers comprehensive physiotherapy and rehabilitation programs.

Objective: Lead Generation and Client Acquisition

- Generate leads and convert them into clients (patients) for MOVENOW's physiotherapy and rehabilitation services.

- Target individuals in Pakistan seeking physiotherapy and rehabilitation solutions for various health concerns.

Challenges Facing:

- Non-user-friendly website limiting lead generation opportunities.

- Lack of engagement and absence on social media platforms, hindering brand visibility and outreach.

- No prior experience in utilizing digital campaigns to attract clients.

- Website issues including unreadable text, lack of prominence for discount information, unprofessional animations, low-quality logo, dummy social media links, and prominent fake reviews affecting credibility.

possible Solution:

- Revise and enhance the website to improve user experience, readability, and professionalism. Address issues such as unreadable text, prominence of discount information, excessive animations, logo quality, and integration of functional social media icons and Google Maps location links.

- Establish a presence on key social media platforms including Facebook, Instagram, and YouTube to increase engagement and reach.

- Launch targeted social media campaigns across platforms to promote MOVENOW's services and attract potential clients.

- Implement aggressive retargeting messaging strategies to capture leads and convert them into clients.

- Proactively monitor and manage online reviews to ensure a positive brand image and reputation.

- Provide support to the MOVENOW team to effectively manage digital campaigns and client interactions.
